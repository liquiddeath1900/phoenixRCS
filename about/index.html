Our Story

Rising From the Ashes

Phoenix RCS was founded with a simple mission: to be there for Maryland homeowners and businesses during their most stressful moments. When water floods your basement at 2 AM, when fire devastates your property, or when you discover dangerous mold - you need a team that responds immediately and gets the job done right.

Based in Lanham, Maryland, our team has spent years building relationships throughout Prince George's County, Montgomery County, and beyond. We've grown from a small local operation to a trusted name serving Maryland, Virginia, and Washington DC.

What sets us apart? We treat every property like it's our own. Our technicians are IICRC certified professionals who take pride in restoring homes and businesses to better-than-before condition. We handle the insurance paperwork so you can focus on what matters most - your family and your life.
