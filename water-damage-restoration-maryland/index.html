Our Proven 6-Step Water Damage Restoration Process

Our systematic approach ensures thorough water damage restoration while minimizing disruption to your life. Every step is documented for insurance purposes:

1. Emergency Response

Within 60 minutes of your call, our certified technicians arrive from our Lanham headquarters with professional equipment to assess the situation and begin immediate water damage mitigation.

2. Damage Assessment

We conduct a thorough inspection using moisture meters, thermal imaging, and hygrometers to determine the full extent of water damage and develop a comprehensive restoration plan tailored to your specific situation.

3. Water Extraction

Using powerful truck-mounted and portable extractors, we remove standing water quickly to prevent further damage. Our industrial-grade equipment can extract thousands of gallons per hour, significantly faster than standard equipment.

4. Structural Drying

We strategically place commercial dehumidifiers and high-velocity air movers to create optimal drying conditions. Maryland's humid climate requires specialized drying techniques - we monitor moisture levels daily until complete dryness is achieved.

5. Cleaning & Sanitizing

All affected areas are cleaned and sanitized using EPA-approved antimicrobial treatments to prevent mold growth and eliminate odors. Personal belongings are cleaned using specialized techniques appropriate for each item type.

6. Restoration

The final step involves restoring your property to pre-damage condition or better. This may include minor repairs like drywall replacement or major reconstruction projects - we handle it all with our licensed contractors.
