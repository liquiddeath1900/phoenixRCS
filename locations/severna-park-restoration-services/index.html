Why Severna Park Properties Need Specialized Restoration

Four challenges unique to this community — and how we handle each one.

1

Waterfront Storm Exposure

Properties along the Severn River and Magothy River face direct storm surge, high winds, and saltwater flooding from Chesapeake Bay weather systems. We deploy saltwater-specific extraction and corrosion prevention protocols within the first hour.

2

Country Club Communities

Severna Park's gated and HOA communities — Chartridge, Olde Severna Park — have specific contractor requirements and access protocols. We carry all required licenses, insurance certificates, and coordinate directly with your HOA board.

3

Mature Tree Damage

The area's dense hardwood canopy creates major roof and structural damage during ice storms and nor'easters. Our crews handle emergency tree-strike board-ups and full structural restoration, from rafters to roofing.

4

Split-Level & Colonial Flooding

The predominant 1960s–1980s split-level and colonial architecture creates unique basement and lower-level flooding patterns. We understand how water travels through these floor plans and position drying equipment accordingly.
