Why Annapolis Needs Specialized Restoration

Annapolis sits where the Severn River meets the Chesapeake Bay. That geography — combined with colonial-era architecture and waterfront living — creates restoration challenges found nowhere else in Maryland.

  • 1
    Salt Air Corrosion — Waterfront and near-waterfront properties suffer accelerated damage from salt air exposure, requiring specialized materials and coatings during restoration.
  • 2
    Chesapeake Bay Storm Surge — Annapolis faces unique flooding risks from nor'easters and tropical systems that push Chesapeake Bay water into low-lying neighborhoods.
  • 3
    Colonial-Era Architecture — The historic district contains some of Maryland's oldest structures (1700s), demanding restoration expertise that preserves irreplaceable heritage.
  • 4
    Marina & Dock Proximity — Many premium properties sit directly on the water, creating complex restoration logistics and moisture control challenges.
