Why Chevy Chase Needs Specialized Restoration

Chevy Chase's century-old estates, mature tree canopy, and historic overlay zones demand restoration expertise that goes beyond standard approaches.

  • 1
    100+ Year-Old Homes — Chevy Chase's turn-of-the-century mansions feature original plaster, knob-and-tube wiring proximity, and irreplaceable architectural details requiring museum-quality restoration care.
  • 2
    Old Money, Old Trees — The community's signature mature tree canopy (100-year-old oaks) causes severe storm damage to roofs, gutters, and foundations every season.
  • 3
    Historic Preservation Requirements — Many Chevy Chase properties fall under historic overlay zones requiring restoration work that maintains original character.
  • 4
    Underground Water Issues — The area's proximity to Rock Creek and aging municipal infrastructure creates chronic water intrusion in basements and crawl spaces.
